Chapter 1 Ending G (Riches and Glory) 

You stealthily scale the wall upwards. Once on the wall, you look down at the castle grounds. 

You see the village people who you stole from. It appears they are setting up a trap connected to the castle gates. 

It is fortunate you chose to scale the wall, or else you may have met an untimely demise. 

You sneak across the castle walls until you see what appears to be a throne room. You go down and enter the  

room. Inside lay an undescribable fortune. However what caught your eye was a blade unlike anything you had ever seen before. 

You grip its polished handle and yank it out of the piles of gold. the sword glows gold. It is the weapon of a warrior, 

not a theif or a scoundrel. You feel the blade enlighten you. No more plundering villages for resources or valuables. 

You decide to use your skillset for good and helping others rather than personal gain. 

Advance to Chapter 2.
