The Sensei 

You tell the sensei that you are honoured, but wish to decline. You refuse as you do not wish to train under some hermit, 

but fight a worthy opponent. If you could defeat someone of great status and power, your honour and respect will be restored. 

You walk away from the sensei, with a blade in your hand, seeking a worthy opponent. 

In the darkness you spot a fierely menacing being, unlike anything you have ever witnessed. His voice deep, his tone menacing, his eys glaring. 

This man was clearly a worthy opponent. A battle with him would be legendary. However you hesitate to challenge him, 

for you may not be able to match his might. Either fight him and reclaim your honour or go back to the sensei and accept his offer. 

CHOOSE YOUR DESTINY!
