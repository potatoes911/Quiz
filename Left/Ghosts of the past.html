Ghosts of Sensei's Past 

You sprint to follow the strange man. He attempts to evade you, but you catch up. He stops running, and turns around, holding Sensei hostage. 

His coarse voice struck fear in your heart. He demanded your Sensei to tell you what was happening. Sensei explains that this man was once one of his 

best pupils in the past, but one day he turned againt Sensei. He thought himself superior to all else due to his great skill in offensive combat. 

Sensei cast him out, so he retaliated against Sensei. Eventually Sensei beat him and spared his life. Sensei explains this to be the reason why he despised violence. 

Sensei also warns you of his prowess and skill in combat. Sensei says he thought his old pupil was long gone, but he has returned. He seeks vengeance against Sensei. You do not wish Sensei to meet his demise. You devise of two strategies to. 

combat this problem. The first to quickly attack him and disarm him before he can make his move. The other option is to talk out your differences.
