Return 

You sprint back to the sensei. He is sitting down, meditating. You approach the Sensei. His eyes slowly open as he gracefully stands up. 

You inform him of the peculiar man you saw, and how you have changed you mind and wish to train under him. The Sensei accepts, however he tells you. 

he wont teach you how to fight, only to survive and defend. Out of curiosity, you ask why he would not teach you how to fight. 

The Sensei informs you that he a great student before you. He tells you he was a progidy. He was unlike anything the sensei had ever seen before. 

His strikes as string as a lion, his speed faster than a leapord, his sight greater than a hawk's, his mind intelligent and his reflexes sharp. 

His last student was going to be his greatest student, until one day something changed in him. His heart. The student felt that he was 

the greatest combatant to ever exist. The Sensei was horrified by what had happened. He exiled his pupil. His apprentice refused to leave, 

telling the Sensei that he was superior to him in every way. Both drew their blades and fought to the end. In the end, the Sensei won, 

however overwhlemed by guilt and sorrow, he could not deal the final blow. His last student left. You tell the Sensei that you are sorry 

for what has happened. The Sensei tell you he is sorry for you, as the ominous man you encountered was his last apprentice.
