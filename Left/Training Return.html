Training 

The Sensei teaches you everything he knows about defense and survival. You spend sunrise to sunset training. The Sensei says that you are 

talented, but it will take more than talent if you wish to be great. You continue spending day after day with the Sensei. 

One day he calls you. He tell you that you have stellarly completed all knows about defense and survival. You ask him to teach you about combat and fighting  

but the Sensei fears what may happen if he does. A few days go by until one day, you wake up to the sight of flames. You see the Sensei holding his katana 

in the courtyard, aimed at his previous apprentice. His previous student burnt down the monestary. Furios, you run down to the courtyard to aid Sensei. 

When you reach the courtyard, you hear the Sensei's previous student mocking Sensei, saying he isn't half the man he is, and that his new apprentice (you) will 

never be able to defeat him. Sensei looks over to you and tells you that this is not your fight. He gives you a weapon and tells you to run.
