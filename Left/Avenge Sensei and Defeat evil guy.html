Chapter 1.5 Ending I (The New Sensei) 

You battle him with the blade Sensei gave you before you ran. You match your opponent well, yet he is far more experienced. 

You still persist to win the conflict, yet eventually you are disarmed. The fallen student admits you were a worthy opponent. 

He says you were good, but not good enough. He lifts his blade up. It reverberates through the air towards you. Right before he can deal. 

the finishing hit, your blade glows green. It hovers through the air and flies towards you. You catch it and continue to fight. 

The blade amplifies your abilities, making your more powerful. After a climactic fight, you eliminate the pupil. You hear a voice 

that resembles Sensei's. It congratulates you. The voice is coming from the blade. You look at the blade and in its reflection you see. 

Sensei. Sensei explains he knew that they would not beat his apprentice together, so he gave him a blade that would absorb Sensei's soul once he died. 

Sensei also explained that without the anger and determination brought by his death, you would not have won that fight. Sensei's soul. 

now resides within the sword. He continues to guide you, until a few days later. He tells you that you have proven worthy to be the next Sensei.
