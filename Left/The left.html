The Sensei 

You venture down the left path into the unknown. You have arrived to this place for a reason. You were once a great warrior, yet now you are merely a shadow of your former self. 

You seek redemption in these mountains. As you amble along, you spot an elderly man with a katana. You are 

fascinated and surprised by his skill with the blade, that even in his elderly age he strikes like a younger man. 

You approach the old man with humility and respect, and compliment his skills with the blade. You inform him that you wish to be 

a great warrior again, as he clearly is. The man tells you he is a sensei. He uses few words yet each word he says has meaning. 

He is a simple yet complex man, whose nature is confusing but very respectable. However you are not sure whether you should trust him. People in these parts can be very dangerous.  

After a bit of talking, the sensei extends his hand. He offers you to be his next pupil.
