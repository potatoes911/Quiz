Chapter 1 Ending C (Warrior's Fate) 

Although it is significantly larger and stronger than you, you do not back down from its offer. 

The monster makes a blade of ice and attempts to swing at you in order to destory you. 

However you are faster and smarter. you dodge the monsters attacks and slash at it until it finally falls. 

Once the monster was defeated, the peaks unfroze. 

Victory is yours.
