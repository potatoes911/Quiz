Chapter 1 Ending E (Vengeance) 

As you swim towards the shore, you hear shouting behind you. You resurface and look behind. 

Behind you is a massive fleet of ships. Aboard the ships are the peop,e of the village that you plundered. 

It turns out they had been tailing you ever since you left. You try to outswim the villagers but their ships 

catch up to you. They grab you and pull you aboard. They say that they will punish you for your crimes, and have their vengeance.
